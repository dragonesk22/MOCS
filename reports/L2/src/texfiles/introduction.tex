\section{Problem statement}

In this lab we will be investigating classical examples of cellular automata. Cellular automata are ...

The goal of the lab is to investigate how simple sets of rules like Defitions (\ref{conwayrules}, \ref{neuronrules} \& \ref{def:GHCArules}) on lattice graphs:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Give rise to discrete dynamical systems, and how different initial conditions affect the state of the system over time.
\item Manifest the causal dependence to complex phenomena like
    \begin{itemize}
    \item Turing completeness
    \item Universal computation (both via. realisation of a Boolean basis)
    \item Simulation of neuron activation
    \item Stability and oscillatory behaviour in lattice-based dynamical systems $S^\Lambda$, where $\Lambda \subset \mathbb{Z}^d$ and $S$ is a finite alphabet (e.g.\ $\{0,1\}$ or $\{\text{firing, resting, ready}\}$), in comparison with dynamical systems on continuous function spaces.
    \end{itemize}
\end{enumerate}
